Schools

Welcome!

Our goal at Forex-Comix is to make language learning fun. We strongly believe that a student's progress can be accelerated when exposed to the diverse range of illustrated comics, books and graphic novels.

Given the public's appetite for superhero movies, the box office success of the Avengers and other movies, interest in comics has never been greater for both adults and children.

Our comic database is the ideal resource to complement your course material in a fun and challenging way.

Membership Benefits

  • 10 comics delivered _every month_ directly to your school/college

  • Keep the comic if you want! Or send them back (SAE included) and we offer credit against your next comic purchases.

  • You choose from our catalogue the language, and desired profile, e.g. children, adults

  • Excellent condition and in paperback. Our research shows that students are less likely to engage with electronic comics versus the real thing. Nothing quite beats the feel of a real comic!

  • Give students a break from the textbooks and introduce a pleasure outlet for their creative mind to explore.

  • Increase retention of your language students by increasing their enjoyment of language learning.

  • Use the comics and content to explore new themes and discussion with your students.

  • Tap into the comic book network to find new students. What is the value of a new signup up to your language course?
