Welcome!

We love comics. We love language learning.

We think foreign language comics are one of the best ways to achieve this aim.

There is a surprisingly small number of foreign language comics in Dublin. For such a multicultural city, the options for language learners, especially for beginners, are typically children's books or novels which are often too advanced.

We want to connect your comic books to an audience who will read widely and often.
