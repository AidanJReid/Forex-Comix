What is Forex Comix?

","","

Not a financial trade term for starters!

","","

Language learning is big business. Most enthusiasts quickly tire of rote grammar books and vocab lists.

","","

Our aim is to make language learning fun, and educational. Impossible? We don't think so.

","","

Graphic novels and comic books are a wonderful way to learn a language - using rich images and conversational styles to entertain children and adults alike.

","","

So whether you pine for some Tintin in the original French, or The Walking Dead in Spanish, Forex-Comix provide a platform to enable exchange with other like-minded comic book fans"," of your target language.

","

How does it work?

","

To participate in an exchange, you should have something to offer. Duh!

","","

Example: I want to learn latin Spanish. I have a few old-school English Marvel comics (The Avengers) that are gathering dust in the attic. Simply, 'Add' the comic to our database. Here you can also see what"," comics are available in your chosen language.

","","

You can exchange comics directly with the owner of the comic of your choice at our monthly networking meetups - also a chance to practice your language skills!).
